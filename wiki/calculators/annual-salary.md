The annual cost associated with an employee's pay and benefits. For calculating total impact of attrition, we recommend leveraging a loaded salary figure which includes the cost of benefits. If your loaded annual salary incorporates costs associated with IT, HR and other support organizations, ensure you do not double count those in [[onboarding-costs]].
