WFM Labs is an organization formed by **workforce management & customer service professionals** seeking to **reinvent the next-generation approach** to successfully operating contact centers. The community examines the workforce management framework in a new light, where **employees are prioritized first** and where **automation & simulation** technologies can be leveraged to benefit employees while delivering superior results to customers & shareholders.
