          Length of Training

          calculator draft May 4, 2026
          capacity-planning contact-center
          migrated-mediawiki

          Length of training is the total time from beginning training class to an employee going into production. It is up to individual organizations and the calculation being leverages as to whether to include nesting periods as part of training length, or as part of production.
